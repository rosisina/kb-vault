### Methodological principles established

- **Blind principle (measurement stage).** Subagents dispatched for diff measurement must read ONLY their assigned files. No access to book, vault assets, wiki, Aurora, or CLAUDE.md context. Neutral descriptive language only; no interpretation vocabulary ("manipulation", "concealment", "은폐", "조작", "tampering"). This protects against confirmation bias.
- **Contextual principle (interpretation stage).** When the main agent synthesizes subagent outputs into pattern reports or claim atoms, context is not optional but required. The book, legal framework, real-world timeline, and pseudonym mapping all inform meaning. Formalized in `CLAUDE.md` section "Measurement vs interpretation" on 2026-04-11.
- **Zettelkasten two-tier structure.** Hub pages (entities, events, regulations, layers) link to atoms. Atoms (`wiki/claims/`) are single-claim single-verdict Luhmann-style notes, each carrying its own Aurora `MERGE` block for direct graph serialization.
- **Pseudonym rule (absolute).** Real names never appear anywhere in the vault, even in `## Verbatim` quotes. The only real↔pseudonym table is `../defense-kg-platform/kg/pseudonym_mapping.json`. Legacy assets under `raw/` are gitignored to prevent real names leaking to git.
- **Record No. dual citation system.** (a) `Record No. NNNNN` (global, 1–13495) — maps to Aurora `:Evidence {docPage}`. (b) `L{N}-NNN` (layer-local) — maps to Aurora `:Evidence {evidenceId}`. Both valid; when they identify the same evidence, write both as `Record No. 10,347 / L4-001`.
- **Regulation-text claim exemption.** Claims derived from regulation text cite the directive number + article number (e.g., `훈령 제2129호 제60조 제1항 제3호`) in lieu of `Record No.` / `L{N}-NNN`. Established during the Phase A calibration ingest.
- **Entity-naming anchors in regulations are Layer-critical.** Explicit naming of a specific organization, facility, or system within a regulation article is more diagnostic than procedural scaffolding around it. Any change to an entity-naming anchor across revisions is a direct manipulation signal. Formalized in `CLAUDE.md`.
