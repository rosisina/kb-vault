# 2018–2019 KIATIS 성능개선사업 (KIATIS Performance Improvement Project)

The **KIATIS 성능개선사업 (KIATIS Performance Improvement Project)** is the specific defense information system project at the center of the 7-layer proof system's Layers 2–7 criminal narrative. It is a pure software development service contract (순수 소프트웨어 개발 용역사업) under the State Contract Act (국가계약법), valued at **6.25억 원 (625 million KRW)**, and was conducted between 2018 and 2019 under 훈령 제2129호 (2018-02-05) as the RFP-named applicable directive. This event page is the factual anchor against which all subsequent Layer 4 and Layer 6 claim atoms must be verified — the "legally eligible window" during which the project's conduct occurred.

## Key Takeaways

- [진리성] **The project is a pure software development service contract** (순수 소프트웨어 개발 용역사업), not a weapon system acquisition, not a commercial product purchase, not a facility construction, not an R&D experiment. This classification means 제2129호 제9조 ¶1 (weapon-system carve-out) **does not apply** to KIATIS.
- [진리성] **Contract value: 6.25억 원.** Above the **5억 원 threshold** in 제2129호 제58조 제3항. Under the statutory scheme, KIATIS therefore falls under the **제58조 제2항 main regime** (국방부 통제사업, separation-of-DT&E-and-OT&E is the default), not the 제58조 제3–4항 delegated regime (simultaneous execution permitted for small projects). See [[../regulations/defense-it-2129-article-58|제58조 analysis]] for the threshold discussion.
- [타당성] **Applicable directive: 훈령 제2129호 (2018-02-05) — single.** The project's 제안요청서 (Request for Proposal) names 제2129호 as the applicable directive. Under 국가계약법, the RFP-named directive binds the project throughout its lifecycle. 제2263호 (2019-02-26) and all subsequent revisions are **not applicable** to KIATIS conduct, even though 시험평가 completed in 2019-09 after 제2263호's effective date — because RFP-time law governs, not project-completion-time law. James explicitly confirmed this: "제2263호는 제가 보수적으로 말한 것입니다."
- [진리성] **Under 제2129호's organizational framework (제11조), KIATIS has three designated roles:**
  - **사업통제기관 (project control agency):** [[../entities/organizations/mnd-it-planning-office|국본 정보화기획관실]] — approves T&E plans and results, per 제11조 ¶2 + 제59조 제4항 + 제60조 제2항 + 제61조 제2항 + 제62조 제5항 + 제64조 routing.
  - **사업관리기관 (project management agency):** [[../entities/organizations/gukjeonwon|국전원 (국방전산정보원)]] — conducts **개발시험평가 (DT&E)** per 제11조 ¶4 제3호 + 제59–61조.
  - **사업주관기관 (project sponsor agency):** [[../entities/organizations/dma-defense-pow-mia-accounting-agency|국방 유해발굴사업단 (DMA)]] — conducts **운용시험평가 (OT&E)** per 제11조 ¶3 제4호 + 제62–64조; issues the `군사용 적합·부적합` military-suitability verdict per 제11조 ¶3 제5호 + 제64조 제1항.
- [타당성] **Under 제2129호, DT&E and OT&E for KIATIS must be separated.** Per 제58조 제2항: `국방부 통제사업의 시험평가 절차는 제59조에서 제64조까지의 내용을 준용하며 개발시험평가와 운용시험평가를 분리하는 것을 원칙으로 하되, 필요시 사업통제기관의 승인을 받아 동시에 실시할 수 있다.` Simultaneous execution would require **written 국본 정보화기획관실 approval** as a 제58조 제2항 exception — an auditable document.
- [타당성] **Project timeline:**
  - **2018**: 사업계획서 (project plan) drafting, 제안요청서 (RFP) issuance, vendor selection (business entity selected by **2018-12**).
  - **2019-01 to 2019-08**: 사업관리 (project management phase under 국전원).
  - **2019-09-01 to 2019-12**: 시험평가 (test evaluation phase) — DT&E and OT&E conducted in this window.
- [진리성] **The 2019-09 test evaluation window is the legally decisive period.** Any claim about whether DT&E/OT&E were properly separated, whether the 국전원-led DT&E results were properly handed off to the DMA-led OT&E, and whether the 국본 정보화기획관실 properly approved both plans and results under 제2129호, must be anchored to events within this window.
- [진리성] **The 2022 MPO investigation targeted this project.** Per James, the 군 검찰단 사기 수사 and 조사본부 사기 감사 of 2022 examined KIATIS conduct from 2018–2019 and labeled James as a 피의자. The investigation's choice of applicable directive is itself a diagnostic question — if the investigation applied post-제2436호 (2020-06-04) revisions to 2018–2019 conduct, that constitutes retroactive (ex post facto) application of norms that did not exist when the conduct occurred. See [[../layers/layer-6|Layer 6]] and the pending claim atom tracking this question.

## The 5억 원 threshold argument

The project's 6.25억 원 contract value forecloses three of the most likely legal paths by which a later investigation could characterize KIATIS as eligible for integrated DT&E/OT&E execution:

| Path | Mechanism | Foreclosed by |
|---|---|---|
| A | 제58조 제3항 — "5억 원 미만" delegated regime | KIATIS contract value is 6.25억 (above threshold) |
| B | 제58조 제4항 — simultaneous DT&E/OT&E in OT&E environment | Available only to 제3항 projects; therefore also foreclosed |
| C | 제58조 제2항 exception — written sponsor approval for simultaneous execution | Requires documented approval from 국본 정보화기획관실; existence/absence is auditable |

Any 2022 investigation claim that KIATIS DT&E/OT&E were legitimately integrated must rely on path C (produce the written approval) or fall back to retroactive application of a later directive.
