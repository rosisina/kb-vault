## Scope rules for topic pages

- **Narrative, not atomic.** Topic pages summarize a concept and link out. Individual falsifiable claims belong in [[../claims/_index|claims]].
- **Can cross layers.** Unlike event/regulation pages, topic pages routinely span all 7 layers when the concept is meta-level (e.g., APT-Style, Cartel, Banality).
- **Derived pages.** Topic pages draw from vault-legacy synthesis + vault-converted book chapters. They do not replace the direct book compile; they are the **reading layer** over it.
- **Author self-reference.** Use "the author" (or "the whistleblower" when legally framed). Do not use personal names. James is not in the pseudonym mapping and this vault does not introduce one for him.
