# 7-Layer Proof System (7층위 증명 체계)

The 7-Layer Proof System is the core analytical framework of the book *Beyond Cybersecurity* (사이버보안을 넘어). It documents the alleged organized crime of the Defense Informatization Cartel through **seven interconnected but independently provable layers of evidence**, supported by approximately **13,495 pages of evidence record** indexed as `Record No. 1` through `Record No. 13,495`. Each layer operates at a different stage of the 2016 DIDC hacking cover-up and its subsequent targeting of the whistleblower.

## The seven layers

### [[../layers/layer-1|Layer 1 — Active-X / Old KIATIS history erasure]]
- **Evidence records:** 1–810
- **Core claim:** The old KIATIS operated exposed on the Internet without VPN for **15 years (2007–2022)**, and its operational history was systematically erased during the Active-X removal project to conceal the root cause of the 2016 DIDC North Korean hacking.
- **Evidence types:** Official documents, RFPs, stakeholder conversations, witness statements.
- **Role in the narrative:** Establishes the foundational motive for the entire cover-up.
- **Source chapter:** `vault-converted-english/11-3-1-31-Layer-1.md`

### [[../layers/layer-2|Layer 2 — New KIATIS project promotion and officer record manipulation]]
- **Evidence records:** 1000–1587
- **Core claim:** Illegal transfer of project control authority from MND to a subordinate host agency, and fabrication of project plans and RFPs; alleged manipulation of officer individual performance records.
- **Evidence types:** Defense Information Technology Operations Directive revisions, project plans, approval documents.
- **Significance:** Documents MND's evasion of oversight duties.
- **Source chapter:** `vault-converted-english/12-3-2-32-Second-Layer.md`

### [[../layers/layer-3|Layer 3 — Assignment to DCIA and SW development project management]]
- **Evidence records:** 1600–2465
- **Core claim:** Creation of a scapegoat structure through position manipulation and duty transfers; work overload through assignment of seven simultaneous projects as a targeting tactic.
- **Evidence types:** Personnel orders, duty assignments, interrogation transcripts.
- **Significance:** Establishes the "many-to-one" attack geometry (see [[apt-style-individual-targeting|APT-Style Individual Targeting]]).
- **Source chapter:** `vault-converted-english/13-3-3-33-Third-Layer.md`

### [[../layers/layer-4|Layer 4 — Systematic manipulation before, during, and after test evaluation]]
- **Evidence records:** 2500–3699
- **Core claim:** The [[../events/2018-2019-kiatis-performance-improvement-project|New KIATIS performance improvement project]] test evaluation environment was systematically manipulated; a KIDA research report was allegedly fabricated; a 99.7-point successful evaluation result was allegedly reversed and documented as failure.
- **Evidence types:** Firewall policies, truth function tables, regulation revision history (see [[../regulations/defense-it-operations-directive-2129|훈령 제2129호]] and its [[../regulations/defense-it-operations-directive-2129#predecessor-diff-제2075호--제2129호-2017-10-10--2018-02-05|forward diff]]), 99.7-point evaluation result.
- **Significance:** The most extensive layer; presents the core physical evidence. Connects directly to the [[test-evaluation-manipulation|Test Evaluation Manipulation]] topic.
- **Source chapter:** `vault-converted-english/14-3-4-34-Fourth-Layer.md`

### [[../layers/layer-5|Layer 5 — False power-abuse report and manipulated audit]]
- **Evidence records:** 3700–4555
- **Core claim:** A false power-abuse (갑질) report was filed **exactly 48 hours after** the 2022-02-08 meeting that exposed old KIATIS's Internet exposure; the Investigation Headquarters (조사본부) then conducted a manipulated audit with documented patterns including solitary-confinement-style isolation, 29 leading questions, and coordinated false statements.
- **Evidence types:** Timeline analysis, solitary confinement records, 29 leading questions, coordinated false statements.
- **Significance:** Establishes the false report as a witness-elimination operation.
- **Source chapter:** `vault-converted-english/15-3-5-35-Fifth-Layer.md`

### [[../layers/layer-6|Layer 6 — MPO's fraud investigation and criminal stigmatization]]
- **Evidence records:** 4600–5248
- **Core claim:** The Military Prosecutor's Office's investigation itself **satisfies the elements of criminal fraud** (see [[fraud-investigation|Fraud Investigation]]), because it was built on the false premise that four distinct KIATIS environments were identical.
- **Evidence types:** Comparison of 4 distinct KIATIS environments, criminal-offense element analysis, tracking charts.
- **Significance:** Proves abuse of investigative authority by a state power agency.
- **Source chapter:** `vault-converted-english/16-3-6-36-Sixth-Layer.md`

### [[../layers/layer-7|Layer 7 — Petitions to oversight bodies and deferred prosecution]]
- **Evidence records:** 5300–13495
- **Core claim:** All petitions to oversight bodies (MND Minister, Military Prosecutor General, National Human Rights Commission, Anti-Corruption and Civil Rights Commission) were ignored or dismissed; the case ended with **deferred prosecution (기소유예)**.
- **Evidence types:** Original petitions, transcript of call with the Chief Prosecutor, agency responses.
- **Significance:** Confirms institutional failure of all oversight mechanisms; drives the reform proposals in [[whistleblower-protection-and-reform|Whistleblower Protection and Institutional Reform]].
- **Source chapter:** `vault-converted-english/17-3-7-37-Seventh-Layer.md`

## Methodology of the proof system

- **Evidence-based discovery** — every layer's claims are anchored to specific `Record No.` citations in the evidence record.
- **Logical proof via truth function tables** — Layer 4 uses mathematical/logical proof to demonstrate that four distinct KIATIS environments cannot be identical.
- **Temporal forensics** — Layers 4 and 5 track backdated document manipulation (the "time reversal" phenomenon; see [[../regulations/defense-it-2129-article-76|제76조 article]]).
- **Independence with interconnection** — each layer can be proven on its own evidence, and the seven layers together form a single chain of causation from the 2016 hacking to the 2023 deferred prosecution.

## Key Takeaways

- [진리성] The proof system documents a four-year (2019–2023) targeted attack against a single whistleblower carried out across seven independently provable institutional layers.
- [진리성] Each layer's evidence is anchored to a specific range of evidence record numbers (1–810, 1000–1587, 1600–2465, 2500–3699, 3700–4555, 4600–5248, 5300–13495), allowing direct traceability from any claim in the book to scanned evidence pages.
- [타당성] Layer 4 is the most extensive and structurally central: it operates on the [[../events/2018-2019-kiatis-performance-improvement-project|KIATIS performance improvement project (6.25억 원, 2018–2019)]] and is governed by [[../regulations/defense-it-operations-directive-2129|훈령 제2129호 (2018-02-05)]] as the RFP-named applicable directive.
- [진실성] Layers 5, 6, 7 document the progressive institutional failures after the whistleblower was identified — from false power-abuse report through fraud investigation to universal oversight-body silence.
- [진리성] The 7-layer framework corresponds directly to Aurora's `:Layer {layerNum: 1..7}` nodes and to the evidence_record_mapping.json ranges — the narrative layer and the graph layer share the same partition.
