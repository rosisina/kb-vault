## Key Takeaways

- [진리성] **Role at KIATIS:** 사업관리팀장 (Project Management Team Leader). Per 한지훈's own account in `raw/02 [011]` (recording 142, 2022-09-28): `제가 사업관리팀장이라는 것만 알면 돼요. 제가 사업관리팀장이고 그 영장에 나와 있는 거에 시험평가환경을 속였다고 하는 모든 것들은 저하고 상관이 없어요. … 사업관리팀장은 절대 평가에 들어가면 안되는 거예요. 담당이 나뉘어져 있어요. 그거는 기술이 아니고 법이에요 법. 훈령에도 나와있고.` — verbatim defense citing the directive's role-tier separation principle. (Original raw/02 [011] persona tag uses real name; redacted to 임형규 per pseudonym mapping.)
- [타당성] **Regulatory basis for the role-separation defense:** [[../../regulations/defense-it-2129-article-11|훈령 제2129호 제11조]] separates 사업통제기관 / 사업주관기관 / 사업관리기관. KIATIS 사업관리기관 = 국전원 (per 제11조 ¶4 explicit naming); 사업주관기관 = 국유단 (DMA). Test-evaluation execution responsibilities per the 11-anchor framework attach to the 사업주관기관 OT&E and 사업관리기관 DT&E roles, not to a "사업관리팀장" position within either.
- [진실성] **Subject of the 2022 prosecutor investigation; the 기소유예 outcome is criminal stigma, not exoneration.** Charges centered on `시험평가환경을 속였다` (lying about the test-evaluation environment). Per `raw/02 [011]`, the 압수수색 (search and seizure) found no contractor collusion: `(군검사 임형규 07:23) 그런데 그거는 말씀하신 데로 뭐. 저번에 압수수색해보니까 업체와 관련 있다 거나 그런 거는 없다는 것.` The disposition was **기소유예** (deferred prosecution) — under Korean criminal procedure, this is a 불기소 처분 of the `범죄혐의가 인정되나` discretionary type, structurally distinct from 무혐의 (no charges) and 기각 (dismissed). 한지훈's own articulation in raw/02 recording 159 line 3540: `내가 왜 기소유예를 당합니까? 제가 문제가 없는데.` and line 3541: `무혐의지. 기소유예는 범죄사실이 있`. **The proper disposition for a contractor-collusion charge with zero contractor-collusion evidence would have been 무혐의, not 기소유예.** See [[../../claims/han-ji-hoon-kiso-yuye-is-criminal-stigma]] for the harm-structure atom.

## The 7-component harm structure (per James 2026-04-11)

The Layer 6 injury to 한지훈 is not a single event but a continuous procedural sequence:

1. **불법적 영장** — unlawful warrant (charge structurally inapplicable to the 사업관리팀장 role per [[../../claims/han-ji-hoon-prosecution-violates-2129-role-separation]])
2. **압수수색** — search and seizure executed under (1)
3. **피의자 조사** — suspect interrogation
4. **기소유예 처분** — final disposition that brands the recipient with `범죄혐의가 인정되나` (criminal facts recognized) despite lawful conduct
5. **평판 손상** — reputation damage to a person who acted lawfully (`올바르게 업무 추진`)
6. **32년 군 경력자에 대한 범죄자 낙인** — criminal stigma on a 32-year military career
7. **가족 피해 + 전역 후 사회적 고립** — family harm + post-discharge social isolation

The harm is the sequence as a whole; 기소유예 is the procedural marker that solidifies it, not the protective endpoint. Layer 7 (진정서 / petition) is the response to this harm structure.
- [진실성] **Career tenure: 31년 at the time of raw/02 recording 220 (2022-02-08), 32년 at the time of the prosecution disposition (2022-10 onward).** `제가 31년을 근무하거든요` (`raw/02` recording 220 line 178) is the self-statement at the moment of the 2022-02-08 KIATIS-exposure meeting. The book (raw/01 §3.6 line 465) uses `32년 정의로운 군생활` in the context of the 기소유예 disposition — one year later, including the 2022 tenure year. Both figures are correct for their respective reference points; the 7-component harm structure uses 32년 to match the book's framing at the disposition moment.
- [진리성] **Author identity is the methodological premise of the entire vault.** raw/01 (the book) and raw/02 (recording logs) are 한지훈's own work product. Treat them as evidentiary primary sources for his perspective and as the integrated 1~13,000+ Record No. proof structure simultaneously.
