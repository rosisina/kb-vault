The military prosecutor (검사) at 국방부 군검찰단 who was assigned the 한지훈 investigation as the **second** prosecutor — he took over in August 2022 from a previous prosecutor who had handled the case from January 2022. - [진리성] **The 단장 (군검찰단장) approves every decision.** Verbatim from same recording: `(군검사) 이게 저 혼자 판단하는 것이 아닙니다. (본인) 그럼 누구 랑 판단하십니까?
