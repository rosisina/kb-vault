## Key Takeaways

- [진리성] **Dual role.** Section chief of 국전원 소프트웨어품질과 (software quality section) **and** chair of the 新KIATIS 개발운영시험평가위원회. The dual role is the structural seam that Layer 4 alleges was used to combine quality oversight with test-evaluation outcome decisions in a single accountability chain inside 국전원.
- [타당성] **Direct relationship to A8b (사업주관기관 erasure).** [[../../regulations/defense-it-2129-article-57|제57조 ¶1 제2호 OT&E definition]] in 제2129호 baseline: `2. 운용시험평가 : 사업주관기관 주관 하에 …`. The OT&E was supposed to be conducted **under the sponsor agency (DMA / 국유단)**, not by 국전원's internal committee chair. The very existence of a "新KIATIS 개발운영시험평가위원장" position **inside 국전원** (the 사업관리기관, not the 사업주관기관) is a Layer 4 evidence point — it indicates that the OT&E function was structurally located in the wrong organization for KIATIS.
- [진실성] **Named in 한지훈's defense.** Per `raw/02 [011]` recording 142, 한지훈 stated: `사업관리팀장은 절대 평가에 들어가면 안되는 거예요. 담당이 나뉘어져 있어요. 그거는 기술이 아니고 법이에요 법.` — The role-tier separation principle that 한지훈 invoked excludes 사업관리팀장 from evaluation, but the 평가위원장 role inside 국전원 is precisely the structural inversion of this separation.
