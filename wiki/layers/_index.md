# Layers — 7층위 증명 체계

The 7-layer proof system from *Beyond Cybersecurity*. Each layer page summarizes the layer's thesis, lists its evidence record range (from `defense-kg-platform/kg/evidence_record_mapping.json`), and links out to the events, entities, regulations, and claims that operate within it. Layers are the organizing spine of the entire vault — every wiki article should belong to at least one layer.
