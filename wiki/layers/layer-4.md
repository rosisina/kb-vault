## Thesis

Layer 4 is the **most heavily documented layer in the vault** and the directive-manipulation epicenter. The 13-anchor framework — eight of which concentrate in the 시험평가 regime (제57조~제65조) of 훈령 제2129호 — traces how MND systematically rewrote test/evaluation rules to retroactively legitimize the manipulated 新KIATIS lifecycle.

The Layer 4 manipulation has three temporal phases per the book's title structure (`전·중·후` = before/during/after): **before** = directive-text rewrites that loosened the legal regime; **during** = test/evaluation execution that violated even the loosened regime; **after** = academic-foundation manipulation (KIDA report) that retrospectively framed the violations as compliant with international standards.

The single most diagnostic finding is the **제2436호 cluster** (2020-06-04): a single directive revision that produces eight separate anchor manipulations concentrated in 제57조~제61조. Six of those eight cluster anchors fall within the 시험평가 regime alone — a co-occurrence pattern far beyond what random revision would produce.
