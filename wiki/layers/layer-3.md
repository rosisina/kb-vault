# Layer 3 — Gukjeonwon SW Project Management Capture (국전원 전속 후 SW개발사업관리 착수·종결)

**Layer:** 3
**Evidence record range:** Record No. 1600–2465

## Thesis

Layer 3 documents the **transfer of KIATIS to 국전원** (국방전산정보원, formerly 국방통합데이터센터의 운영기관) for SW development project management, and the structural-cartel coordination that the transfer enabled. The transfer is the mechanism by which the post-Layer-2 personnel topology was operationalized: 국전원, as 사업관리기관 under 훈령 제2129호 제11조, became the institutional locus that coordinated the development, operation, and (eventually) test/evaluation manipulation of 新KIATIS without external oversight.

Two distinct manipulation patterns operate at Layer 3: (a) **role-tier renaming** in 훈령 제2129호 제11조 at 제2436호 (2020-06-04) that retiered the 사업통제/사업주관/사업관리 framework into a 정보화기획관실/소요제기/집행 framework — broadening the role boundaries that constrained 국전원, and (b) **substantive cartel coordination** between 5 named organizations as the 한지훈 rebuttal explicitly defines (국방부 검찰단 / 정보화조직 / 국군방첩사령부 / KIDA / 기타).
