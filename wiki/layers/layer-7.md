## Thesis

Layer 7 is the **institutional rejection chain layer**: 한지훈 submitted a 2022-09-25 rebuttal documenting the Layer 1–6 cover-up, and **8 institutions in sequence** rejected it without substantive review — 국방장관 → 군사보좌관 → 군검찰단장 → 검사 → 수사관 → 인권담당 → 국가인권위원회 → 국가권익위원회. The rejection chain is the Layer 7 thesis: every external accountability mechanism South Korea's defense and human-rights systems offer was attempted, and every one failed to function.

Layer 7 is the **largest record range in the vault** — Record No. 5300 through 13495, roughly 60% of the entire 13,495-record corpus. Most of these records document the institutional rejection chain itself: petition submissions, rejection letters, internal audit responses, FOIA requests, and follow-up correspondence. The volume reflects 한지훈's exhaustion of every available remedy.

The **권익위 institutional capture** finding is one of the most diagnostic Layer 7 atoms: a 권익위 실무자 attempted to transfer 한지훈's evidence to MND (the respondent) during a 30-minute recorded call on 2022-10-04. The transferee being the respondent is direct evidence of institutional capture — the supposed accountability mechanism was operating as an extension of the institution being held accountable.

The **인권위 procedural-duty violation** is the second diagnostic Layer 7 atom: 인권위 issued a formal rejection (기록 제5,679~5,680쪽) without calling any witness or reviewing the thousands of evidence pages submitted. This is procedural rejection-by-default rather than substantive review.
