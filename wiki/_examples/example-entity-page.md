# Andrej Karpathy

**Source:** raw/karpathy-llm-knowledge-bases-tweet.md

Researcher and educator known for his work on neural networks, computer vision, and practical AI systems. Former Director of AI at Tesla and founding team member at OpenAI. Posted the LLM knowledge base pattern that this vault template implements.

## Key Takeaways

- Proposed the LLM-maintained personal wiki pattern in April 2026 as an alternative to vector RAG for solo research use cases.
- Runs his own research wiki at approximately 100 articles and 400,000 words, maintained entirely by Claude Code with no vector database.
- Frames Obsidian as the IDE and the LLM as the programmer. The wiki is the codebase.
- Credits Vannevar Bush's 1945 Memex concept as the conceptual ancestor of the pattern.
