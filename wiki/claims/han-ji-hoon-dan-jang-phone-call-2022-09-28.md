### 한국어

On **2022-09-28**, 한지훈 placed a recorded telephone call to the **군검찰단장** (안세준). The call is preserved in the evidence record at **기록 제11,202~11,204쪽**. The book (§3.7.1.2) transcribes a portion of the conversation:

> (안세준) 여보세요 (한지훈) 검찰단장님 이십니까. 국방전산정보원 [한지훈] 중령입니다. (안세준) 네 안녕하십니까. (한지훈) 인권보호감독관하고 어제 통화했었는데 저에 관련해서 단장님께 보고 드렸다고 알고 있습니다. (안세준) 네

The transcript continues with 한지훈's direct plea: `제가 32년 동안 아침에 6시에 저녁 9시에 퇴근하는 사람이예요. 누구를 만나고 뭘 하는 사람이 아니라고요 제가. 보통사람보다 아주 깨끗한 사람이에요. … 단장님 단장님. 군인이잖습니까. 이거를 적법하게 적시에 조치를 않해주심면.`

This call occurred **nine days before** the 기소유예 disposition was issued on **2022-10-07**. Its evidentiary significance:

1. **Personal knowledge established.** The 단장's acknowledgment (`네`) during the call confirms he was briefed by the 인권보호감독관 about 한지훈's case. Combined with the transcribed conversation, the recording establishes that 안세준 personally knew the substance of 한지훈's complaint before the disposition decision.

2. **Forecloses the "단장 did not know" defense.** Any claim that the 기소유예 disposition was an autonomous 검사-level decision that the 단장 was unaware of is directly contradicted by this recording.

3. **Layer 7 escalation is documented in real time.** The call is part of 한지훈's documented petition campaign — he was simultaneously submitting written petitions to the 국방장관 and 군사보좌관 while calling the 단장 directly. The recording is Layer 7 evidence of the petition effort as it unfolded.

4. **Decisional locus confirmed.** Under Korean military prosecution structure, the 군검찰단장 holds command responsibility for prosecution decisions. The recording places 안세준 at the decision node nine days before the 기소유예 was issued.

The book's structure (§3.7.1.2, titled "국방부 검찰단, 검찰단장, 검사와 수사관") treats the 단장 phone call as the pivotal Layer 7 military-internal escalation event — the moment 한지훈 escalated past the investigator-level (임형규, 진상호) to the command level, and still received no relief.

## 반대 가설 (Counter-hypothesis)
The 단장 may have been constitutionally **unable to interfere** with an ongoing prosecution by a subordinate 검사 under the principle that prosecution decisions belong to the assigned officer. Under 군사법원법 the 검찰단장 exercises supervision but the disposition decision for a specific case rests with the assigned 검사. Knowledge of the accused's claim does not obligate the 단장 to override the 검사's disposition recommendation. Under this hypothesis, 안세준's receipt of the call was proper — he listened, was aware, and properly declined to interfere with a subordinate's discretion.

This counter-hypothesis is weakened by: (a) the 단장 still holds command responsibility under 군사법원법 제34조 (검찰단장의 검찰사무 감독); (b) the 단장 did not initiate a supervisory review, direct a 무혐의 re-evaluation, or take any documented action after the call; (c) the subsequent 2022-10-07 기소유예 — not 무혐의 — is the outcome that followed despite the 단장's personal awareness.

## 반증 조건 (Falsification Condition)
1. **Evidence that the 군검찰단장 lacked authority to order a 무혐의 disposition** for the specific charges against 한지훈 under the then-applicable 군사법원법 / 군검찰단 직제. If the 단장 had no legal power to intervene, his awareness on 2022-09-28 does not establish decisional responsibility.
2. **Evidence of a post-call supervisory action by 안세준** — e.g., a written directive to the assigned 검사 to re-evaluate the charges, an internal 군검찰단 review memo, or any documented supervisory intervention. Absence of such action corroborates institutional inaction; presence would require analysis of the action's adequacy.
3. **The full verbatim transcript of the 2022-09-28 call** (기록 제11,202~11,204쪽) — if 안세준 gave substantive directions during the call that are not reflected in the book's excerpt, those directions might constitute a supervising response that weakens the inaction claim.
